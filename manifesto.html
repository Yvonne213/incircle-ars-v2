Artist Manifesto

The person who stands in the circle is an artist.


One Circle

The person who acknowledges themselves as an artist is an artist.

The person whose work is their baby is an artist.

The person who can identify artists is an artist.

The person who has a professional artist statement is an artist.

The person who has extremely high demands on themselves is an artist.

The person who is free from repeating daily work is an artist.

The person who creates spontaneously instead of following the trends is an artist.


Big Circle

The person who knows who they are is an artist.

The person who feels fulfilled is an artist.

The person who stands on the shoulders of giants is an artist.

The person who has their own cosmology is an artist.

The person who always knows that they are inadequate is an artist.

The person who is known to the public online and offline is an artist.

The person who doesn't care about being an artist is an artist.

The person who often struggles with their heart is an artist.

The person who knows how to resist prevailing norms is an artist.

The person who knows how to resist prevailing norms is an artist.
